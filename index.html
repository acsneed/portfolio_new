About me

I'm a Data Engineer with nearly two years of experience designing and optimizing scalable data architectures. Currently at UNCOMN, I hold an Active Secret Security Clearance and specialize in developing robust data pipelines, ETL processes, and advanced visualizations to turn complex datasets into actionable business intelligence.

I hold a Bachelor’s in Data Science from Maryville University and am currently completing a Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence. My background combines a strong foundation in statistical analysis with hands-on expertise in building Medallion Architecture pipelines and migrating legacy systems into modern cloud environments like Databricks.
